## 三、单音节词说明

> ⚠️ **单音节词 (Monosyllabic Words) 按语言学原理不拆分**

**产品呈现逻辑 (Phase 5 武器库)：**

当老师点击"拆音节"按钮时：

| 词类 | 示例 | 系统行为 |
|------|------|----------|
| 多音节词 | `active` | 系统会把单词切开，显示为 `ac` - `tive`，帮助学生分段拼读 |
| 单音节词 | `trust`, `shy`, `join`, `skill` | 系统会整体高亮或保持原样，告诉学生"这是一个不可分割的整体，要一口气读出来" |

**本表中的单音节词：**
- shy
- join
- skill
- trust

## 四、Mode A - 剧情式微阅读 (Story Mode)

### 设计依据

- **分簇策略**：30 个词被智能分为"社交"与"成长"两个簇
- **脚手架**：目标词 **加粗 (Bold)**，句式简单（Max 2 clauses），配 1 道事实核查题

### Story 01：社交篇 (Social Life)

**包含目标词：** introduce, shy, polite, invite, accept, join, active, communicate, honest, trust, secret, realize, decision

**AI 生成文本：**

> Tom was a new student. He was too **shy** to **introduce** himself to the class. But Mary was very **polite**. She decided to **invite** Tom to lunch. Tom was happy to **accept**. He decided to **join** the conversation. They started to **communicate** about their hobbies. Tom **realized** that Mary was an **honest** girl. He could **trust** her with his **secret**. Finally, Tom made a **decision** to become an **active** member of the class.

**验证题：**

| 问题 | 选项 |
|------|------|
| Q: Who invited Tom to lunch? | A. Mary ✅ (Correct) <br> B. A teacher |

---

### Story 02：成长篇 (Growth & Sports)

**包含目标词：** adapt, environment, nervous, challenge, practice, improve, skill, confident, success, celebrate, necessary, avoid, trouble, valuable, solution, suggest

**AI 生成文本：**

> It takes time to **adapt** to a new **environment**. Before the soccer game, Tom felt **nervous**. It was a big **challenge**. The coach **suggested** that it was **necessary** to **practice** more. Tom tried hard to **improve** his **skill**. He wanted to **avoid** making **trouble** for the team. He found a **solution** to run faster. On the game day, he felt **confident**. The team achieved great **success**. It was a **valuable** victory, and they decided to **celebrate** together.

**验证题：**

| 问题 | 选项 |
|------|------|
| Q: How did Tom feel on the game day? | A. Nervous <br> B. Confident ✅ (Correct) |

## 五、Mode B - 仿真完形填空 (Cloze Test)

### 设计依据

- **抽样逻辑**：从 30 个词中抽取 4 个高危词（Environment, Trouble, Communicate, Valuable）进行挖空
- **干扰项逻辑**：严格执行"1 正确 + 1 强干扰 + 2 弱干扰"策略
- **篇幅**：100-120 词，首句不挖空

### 语篇内容

**Topic: Surviving in the Wild**

> Animals live in a wild world. They must learn to change their habits to fit their new **[1]**. For example, when winter comes, it is hard to find food. If animals do not move or sleep, they might be in big **[2]**.
>
> Also, living together is important. Wolves use sounds to **[3]** with each other. This helps them hunt and stay safe. Helping each other is a **[4]** skill for survival.

### 题目配置

| 题号 | 考察词 | 选项配置 | 解析 |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| [1] | environment (n. 环境) | A. government <br> B. retirement <br> C. environment ✅ <br> D. decision | 线索是前文的 "wild world" 和后文的 "winter comes"，指生存环境。 |
| [2] | trouble (n. 麻烦/困难) | A. double <br> B. bubble <br> C. success <br> D. trouble ✅ | 语境逻辑推理：找不到食物会导致困难，而不是成功。 |
| [3] | communicate (v. 交流) | A. communicate ✅ <br> B. celebrate <br> C. practice <br> D. invite | 核心线索是 "use sounds"，声音是交流的媒介。 |
| [4] | valuable (adj. 宝贵的) | A. nervous <br> B. valuable ✅ <br> C. shy <br> D. active | 帮助彼此能 "stay safe"，这显然是有价值 (valuable) 的技能。 |
